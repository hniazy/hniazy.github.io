My Final Project: Gem Game


This is Gem Game I wrote for my final project. In this game you collect gems by Bitmoji,
where you can move the Bitmoji by moving your mouse, you have three lives, this means if you miss 3 gems the game will be over,
the more you collect the gems, the higher the level goes higher and it become harder, you have timer to watch your time,
you can pause the game anytime you want by clicking the pause button, and resume again by clicking resume button.
when the game over you will be able to see your score.
Remember! each gem worth $ 12. Not real money though but game money, don't call me for cash hehe, it is for fun.

HIT ON START TO START THE GAME AND ENJOY IT!!!
